Vehicles

The effectiveness of organizing freight transportation by various modes of transport has long been proven and does not need any confirmation. Today it is difficult to imagine the life of the country without the transportation of goods, and therefore without freight vehicles. Therefore, engineers are creating more and more new and more modern types of freight transport, which have different purposes, operating principles and can be used in different environments.

EUROFUR

This is a tilt vehicle with a semi-trailer with a total carrying capacity of 20-25 tons. It is most often used to transport most types of cargo

AUTO HITCH

This is a truck with a body and a trailer that transports cargo weighing from 16 to 25 tons.

ISOTHERMAL VANS

A special type of truck designed for transporting food. The inside of the van is equipped with climate control that can maintain the temperature at a certain level. Large range of types with lifting capacity from 3 to 25 tons.

REFRIGERATOR OR SEMI TRAILER REFRIGERATOR

This is a truck with a van for transporting perishable products. The temperature inside is maintained from + 25 degrees Celsius to - 25 degrees Celsius. Average load capacity from 12 to 22 tons.

TANK TRUCKS

A special type of trucks that have a narrow purpose and are used for the transportation of non-food and food liquid cargo.

OPEN PLATFORMS

Used for oversized cargo that is resistant to temperature changes. The average lifting capacity of an open platform is from 15 to 20 tons. Open platforms can be converted into car transporters, special trawls for transporting special equipment, and so on.
