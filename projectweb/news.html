Using drones

Drone delivery is a delivery method in which food, medicine, and other goods are transported using commercial drones. Most proposed solutions at the moment involve using a copter with six or more propellers.

Delivery by Parachute

t is assumed that boxes with goods will be attached to the unmanned aerial vehicle as before. But in order not to waste time and energy on bringing the drone to the ground, it will simply drop the package while flying over the recipient's house. In addition to the parachute, the system will use a spring, electromagnets and other mechanisms that will forcibly push the package out of the fastenings.

Electric van

The machine was built to fulfill the “Climate Promise” given by URBAN TRADING. Its essence is that by 2040 the corporation will completely rebuild its business and abandon the use of fossil hydrocarbons, the combustion of which leads to an increase in the share of greenhouse carbon dioxide in the atmosphere.
